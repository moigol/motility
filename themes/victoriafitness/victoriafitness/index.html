About Victoria

Welcome! My name is Victoria, and I am a certified ISSA (International Sports Sciences Association) personal trainer. I began my fitness journey 3 years ago and I have been obsessed with fitness ever since. I got started because I did not feel confident in my body. I have always struggled with developing muscle, and at that time my body had very little definition. I was eager to make a change, one that would make me feel beautiful and strong, and that is when I decided that I would give weight training a try. It proved to be one of the best decisions of my life, and since that day I have been in love with working out! Although I may have started my journey for superficial reasons, I choose to continue along this journey because it has become a lifestyle that has positively changed who I am! Through fitness I have developed as a woman in the best way possible, I have undergone a healthy physical and mental transformation. I have developed as immense amount of self-confident, self-appreciation and self-love throughout this process.
